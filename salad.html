Green Salad with Balsamic Vinaigrette

Ready in: 10 min

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 2 pinches kosher salt
  • 6 tbsp olive oil
  • 8 cups salad greens
  • 1/4 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese crumbles
  • 1/4 cup slivered almonds

Steps:

  1. In a medium bowl, place 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar and 2 pinches kosher salt. Gradually whisk in 6 tablespoons olive oil until the dressing is creamy and emulsified.
  2. Dress 8 cups salad greens with the desired amount of dressing; reserve the rest for future use.
  3. On each plate, place 2 cups salad greens and sprinkle with dried fruit, cheese crumbles, and nuts.
